    Instructions

    1. Download the inventory count form. It contains the list of items per category and the expected count from the system.
    2. Print the form and bring it to the store and the warehouse.
    3. Count the items and write the actual count on the form.
    4. After counting, determine the items with variances.
    5. Verify the quantity of these items by doing a recount.
    6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until either there are no variances or that the variance have been validated.
    7. Create a stock adjustment request for each items with variances.
    8. Submit the stock adjustment request for approval.
    9. Let the admin perform the approved requests.
